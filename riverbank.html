RiverBank

Get paid for your data.

  • Your data is a new asset class – like your bank account. Your car. Your house. Your ten-season box set of Friends.
  • RiverBank is a marketplace that connects the data you create with everyone – business, Big Tech, and advertisers – interested in paying you for it.
  • Convert your data – only the parts you want to sell – into real money.
  • The possibilities are endless, and there's no wrong way to safely share your data.
